Drill for Reading Styles

Instructions: Carefully read the given descriptions and scenarios then identify which reading style each is pertaining to.

SKM - if it describes Skimming.

SCN - if it describes Scanning.

IR - if it describes Intensive Reading.

(1) Finishing the last chapter of Ibong Adarna for a book report.

(2) Learning the step-by-step procedure on how to do a square knot for camping.

(3) Using a cookbook for preparing your next meal.

(4) Reading and analyzing a myth to be able to answer the guide questions.

(5) Josie reads her contract with the company before signing it.

(6) Using the dictionary to look up an unfamiliar word.

(7) Mel goes to the school’s website to look for their contact information

(8) Using Google Search to look for another word for “wise.”

(9) Looking for answers to wh- questions.

(10) Fe goes through the list of COVID-19 symptoms to check if she needs to self-quarantine.

(11) Looking for your name amongst the list of honors.

(12) Lynn read the synopsis of The Hunger Games before deciding to purchase it.

(13) Going over club descriptions before determining what club suits you.

(14) You read for the purpose of understanding the main topic or a thesis statement.

(15) Finding the answer to “What was the idea behind The Giver?”

(16) Analyzing abstracts in determining useful literature for your study.

(17) Leaves out the specifics and focuses on general information.
